About Cookflix

Cookflix is a simple, easy to use recipe sharing website that allows people to share and look up video recipes. We aim to bring together a community for sharing and appreciating good and authentic food from and the world. is unique in a way that allows food enthusiasts with none to high level of expertise in making food to come together and appreciate different food cultures in a very flexible and simple web experience.
